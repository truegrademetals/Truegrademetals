Overview

As a leading supplier & manufacturer in China, TrueGrade Metals supply cost-effective Stellite 19 Products.

Stellite 19 is resistant to severe shock and has excellent redhardness for applications such as cutting tools, machinery parts, high temperature bearing races, nozzle discs, and burnishing rollers.

One of the outstanding properties of Stellite 19 is its ability to remain hard at red heat. This denotes a range of temperatures from 932 to 1562°F. Stellite 19 has impact resistance that is comparable to high-speed steels but has much higher red hardness.

Stellite 19 can be used at higher speeds than high-speed steels. Its excellent resistance to corrosion from many common chemicals combined with its abrasion-resistant qualities makes the use of this alloy economical for machining such materials as plastics and rubber, which liberate corrosives that are used in their manufacture.
