Inconel 718SPF Sheet Supplier

As a leading Inconel materials supplier in China, TrueGrade Metals manufactures and produces cost-effective Inconel 718SPF sheet.

INCONEL® alloy 718SPF is a specifically "tailored" version of INCONEL alloy 718, designed for the superplastic forming process. The alloy is optimized for the process by rigorous control of composition, melt practice and rolling conditions.

As a very common nickel alloy product, Inconel 718SPF sheet is formed by flattening and shearing cold-rolled coil. The thickness of the Inconel 718SPF cold-rolled sheet is generally less than 5mm. Unlike plate, Inconel 718SPF sheets have a brighter finish. The Inconel 718SPF sheet has high strength and good creep resistance at any temperature. It is more fatigue resistant than ordinary alloy sheet. In addition, oxidation resistance is also one of its excellent properties.
